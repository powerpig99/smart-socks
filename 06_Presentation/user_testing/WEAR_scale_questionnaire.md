### Study Context

Thank you for participating in our user study for Smart Socks - a wearable device that recognizes physical activities using textile pressure sensors.

**Please wear the Smart Socks prototype and perform the following activities:**
1. Walk forward and backward (2 minutes)
2. Climb stairs up and down (2 minutes)
3. Sit on a chair (both feet on floor, then cross-legged) (1 minute)
4. Stand upright and lean left/right (1 minute)
5. Perform sit-to-stand transitions (1 minute)

**After completing the activities, please rate your agreement with the following statements.**

---

### Instructions
